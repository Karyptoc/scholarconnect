4. Client Terms

Order Placement: Clients may post academic writing assignments specifying pages (1–100), subject, academic level, deadline, and detailed instructions.

Pricing: Standard orders (deadline ≥5 days) are priced at $10 per page. Urgent orders (deadline <5 days) are priced at $15 per page. These rates may be adjusted by the Platform at any time for new orders.

Deposit: A 50% deposit of the total order value is required upfront. This deposit is held in escrow until order completion. The remaining 50% is released upon client approval.

Revisions: Clients are entitled to up to 2 free revisions per order. Additional revisions may incur additional charges at the Platform's discretion.

Auto-Approval: If a client does not review, approve, dispute, or request revision within 72 hours of delivery, the order is automatically marked as complete and payment released to the writer.

Refunds: Refunds are issued only in cases of non-delivery, verified fraud, or at admin discretion following dispute resolution. The deposit is non-refundable once a writer has been assigned and has begun work, except in dispute outcomes favorable to the client.

Earnings: Writers earn a fixed rate of $4 per page regardless of the client pricing (standard or urgent). Payment is released from escrow after client approval or 72-hour auto-approval.

Maximum Active Orders: Writers may not have more than 3 active orders simultaneously to ensure quality delivery.

Deadlines: Writers are responsible for meeting agreed deadlines. Consistent failure to deliver on time may result in account suspension or permanent banning.

Withdrawals: Minimum withdrawal is $20. Withdrawals are processed via PayPal, M-Pesa, or Bank Transfer within 48 business hours of admin approval.
