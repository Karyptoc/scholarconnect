Pricing FAQ

Is the deposit refundable?

Yes — if no writer is assigned within 24 hours, your deposit is fully refunded. See our Refund Policy.

What counts as one page?

One page = 275 words, double-spaced, 12pt Times New Roman (standard academic format).

Are revisions free?

Yes — every order includes 2 free revisions within 7 days of delivery.

When is my work auto-approved?

If you don't approve or request a revision within 72 hours of delivery, the order is auto-approved and payment released.
